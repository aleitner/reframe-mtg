# Privacy Policy

**Reframe MTG** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What the extension does

- Stores your preferences (frame priority, set ordering, blocked printings) locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage API.
- Sends requests to the [Scryfall API](https://scryfall.com/docs/api) to look up card printing data. These requests contain only card names — no personal or identifying information.

## What the extension does NOT do

- Collect personally identifiable information
- Track browsing activity
- Use analytics or telemetry
- Transmit data to any server other than the Scryfall API for card lookups

## Third-party services

- **Scryfall API** — Used solely to retrieve card printing information. Subject to [Scryfall's privacy policy](https://scryfall.com/docs/privacy).

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [github.com/aleitner/reframe-mtg](https://github.com/aleitner/reframe-mtg/issues).
